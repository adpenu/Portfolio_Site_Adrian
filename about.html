My Story

Hi! I’m Adrian, UX Designer and Frontend Developer with a background in Dentistry and Arts. These may seem like random choices but I find great pleasure in taking on new challenges and finding creative solutions that improve people’s lives, whether that is in the form of a new smile, an oil painting or a web app. I enjoy the process of building a product from start to finish and applying my technical skills to make it functional and appealing. This led me to my current path as a UX Engineer where I can express my ideas in the form of design and develop them for customers.
